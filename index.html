Who We Are

Mission

Our mission is to provide our services around the world. Thus we want to build a world free of illiteracy and unemployment.

Vision

Our vision is to build a world, free of poverty and unemployment and illiteracy by making people self-reliant and well-educated.

Promise

With our due work, we desire to make a bright world; Which will be completely free of illiterate and unemployed.

Duties
of A Member

As a monthly grant prescribed by our organization, each member has to pay a minimum amount which is fixed by each country team, and a maximum of what you can afford. You must be active in your e-mail, our social media platform, and also our website. You must attend the annual meeting in your zone. If there is any program of our organization in your area, you should be present there. If you are a member of the organization, you need to add at least one active member in every three months.
